Kimbap (Seaweed Rice Rolls) Ingredients: Steps:

Description:

Kimbap is a classic Korean seaweed rice roll filled with seasoned beef, vegetables, omelette strips, and pickled radish. It is often packed for lunch and snacks at school and at work. This savory dish is enjoyed by many, from kids to adults. This recipe demonstrates how to create this delicious rice snack, coming from justonecookbook.com

Time needed:

Preparation time - 1 hour and 15 minutes

Cooking time - 30 minutes

Total time - 1 hour and 45 minutes

  • Nori (dried laver seaweed) sheets (good-quality)
  • Cooked short-grain white rice (1 cup of cooked rice per roll)
  • Protein (bulgogi-style seasoned beef and egg omelette strips)
  • Cooked and seasoned carrot and spinach and pickled daikon
  • Toasted sesame oil
  1. Soak and cook the rice. After you wash the rice, soak it for 20–30 minutes to help absorb water into the core of the kernel.
  2. Marinate the beef. Toss the meat in the marinade and allow it to sit while you prepare the other fillings.
  3. Prepare the fillings. Fry the omelette, pan-fry the carrot and spinach, pan-fry the beef, cut the pickled daikon radish.
  4. Roll the Kimbap. Spread a thin layer of cooked rice on the nori sheet, neatly add the fillings, and roll with a bamboo mat.
  5. Brush with sesame oil on top and sprinkle with sesame seeds.
  6. Cut and serve at room temperature.

Servings: 6
